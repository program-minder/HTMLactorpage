VIJAY

Vijay's salary varies depending on the film. For his last film, "GOAT", he reportedly charged ₹200 crore, according to the Times of India. For his upcoming film, "Thalapathy 69", he is reportedly earning ₹275 crore, making him the highest-paid Indian actor, reports the Times of India. In the past, he earned ₹100 crore for "Beast" and an estimated ₹120-150 crore for "Varisu" according to Wikipedia, reports the Times of India.
